Task Distribution

The Icon-QA dataset consists of 3 different tasks:
  • Image MCQ: choose from 2-5 image choices
  • Text MCQ: choose from 2-5 text choices
  • Fill-in-the-blank: short textual answers

The Icon-QA dataset involves questions that require a variety of perceptual and cognitive skills.
